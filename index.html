Unicorn vs. Nightmares

A polished unicorn runner with rainbow shots, flying, gentler level 1 pacing, a tornado boss fight after about 50 seconds, and a much louder boss theme when the showdown begins.
Lives: 7
Score: 0
Best: 0
Boss HP: --
State: Start Screen
Controls: Left/Right arrows move, Space jumps and also lets the unicorn flap/fly upward if you keep pressing it, F shoots a rainbow, P pauses, Enter starts or restarts.
To tune the game, edit the CONFIG object near the top of the script. Boss settings are grouped together so you can tweak health, timing, and attack speed.
